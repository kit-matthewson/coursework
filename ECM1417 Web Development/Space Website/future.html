Future

Timeline

Launch of Intuitive Machines 4

Intuitive Machines 4 (CP-22) is a lander mission to the south pole region of the Moon, currently planned for the Mons Mouton region, scheduled for 2027. It will carry a suite of six science payloads, with a total mass of 79 kg, to the surface.

Launch of Dragonfly to Titan

Dragonfly is a rotorcraft mission to explore Saturn's moon Titan by making flights to various sites separated by tens of kilometers and making measurements over a period of more than two years. The primary goal is to explore the chemistry and habitability of multiple surface sites covering a large area. It aims to launch in 2028.

Pioneer 11 Lamda Aquila Flyby

In 4,000,000 years Pioneer 11 will make a close approach to the star Lamda Aquila, located around 125 light years from Earth.
